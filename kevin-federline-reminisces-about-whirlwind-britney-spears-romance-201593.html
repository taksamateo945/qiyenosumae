Kevin Federline Reminisces About &quot;Whirlwind&quot; Britney Spears Romance

Britney and Kevin: No Longer Chaotic! Britney Spears and ex-husband Kevin Federline spent a few very tumultuous years together back in the early aughts. From their surprise wedding to their UPN reality show to their contentious divorce, custody battle, and Spears' mental breakdown, the couple's relationship was like her 2008 album — a Circus. These days, though, the craziness between them is just a memory.

PHOTOS: Hollywood's friendliest exes

"That time, it's like a whirlwind,” Federline, 36, told Access Hollywood Live hosts Billy Bush and Kit Hoover of his 2004 to 2007 marriage to the "Work Bitch" singer. "You know, you're thrown into this limelight…Everything kinda happened."

Which is not to say it was bad. They had their problems, of course, but they also had two adorable sons, Sean, 9, and Jayden, 8. (Spears, 33, gave birth to their second child two months before she filed for divorce.)

"I mean, it was a good time in my life," the aspiring DJ recalled. "We have two beautiful children. The way that everything has turned out, everybody's happy. Co-parenting is going great. I can't complain."

Indeed, despite some early fighting over the kids after their split — which culminated in the singer's 2007 meltdown, complete with a head-shaving incident and rehab — Federline and Spears seem to be in a good, peaceful place. They've worked out a schedule for the boys over the years, but they also try to be accommodating of each other's busy careers.
