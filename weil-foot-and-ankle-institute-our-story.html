Weil Foot &amp; Ankle Institute - Our Story

Together, Drs. Weil Jr., Borelli, Benton-Weil, and Weil Sr. pioneered two life-changing surgeries that offered patients an easier surgery, as well as an easier recovery. The first was the Scarf Bunionectomy, which re-invented the way painful bunions are treated, and improved the recovery after surgery. The second was the Weil Osteotomy, which relieved pain in the ball of the foot, using technologies and procedures previously only used in shoulder replacements.

NEW HEADQUARTERS FOR A NEW DECADE

In the fall of 2019, WFAI moved into their new headquarters – a spacious, modern, and well-equipped office, located in Mount Prospect, IL. Along with providing a more comfortable patient experience, WFAI added both a pathology lab and a SWIFT microwave on-site for an even-better patient experience.

A promise from Dr. Weil and WFAI – “We will never give up on the pursuit of excellence, because it is what allows us to provide a better patient experience.”
