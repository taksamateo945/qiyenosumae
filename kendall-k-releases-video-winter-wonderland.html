Kendall Vertes Releases Video For Winter Wonderland

Kendall Vertes, who is also known by her stage name Kendall K, just released a brand new Christmas music video for her cover of Winter Wonderland!

The 13-year-old singer and dancer is mostly known from the reality show Dance Moms where she and her mother, Jill Vertes, are a part of the main cast. Kendall K started her music career earlier this year with her first single Wear ‘Em Out which was released on April 21. The video for Wear ‘Em Out premiered on an episode of Dance Moms on June 9, and the video went straight to the number one spot on the iTunes music videos chart for all genres. The video has over five million views on YouTube at the moment. Kendall K does not have an album out yet, but according to her team she is working on it in between dance rehearsal and filming her television show.

Kendall K is not the only Dance Moms star who just released a Christmas song. Her younger co-star Mackenzie Ziegler, also known as Mack Z, released a cover of Sleigh Ride in late November.

With recent speculation that Dance Moms may be coming to an end, it looks like Kendall K is trying to branch out of her reality tv role and gain a bigger audience for herself. This week she opened a YouTube account where she plans on interacting with her fans by posting videos regularly. Her first makeup tutorial already has 125.000 views!
