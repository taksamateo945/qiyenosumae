The B/R Wrestling Staff Predictions for AEW Revolution 2024 Match Card | News, Scores, Highlights, S

Orange Cassidy vs. Roddy Strong

OC has held the title for most of its history. Offer a reason Roddy should win this weekend.

The Undisputed Kingdom has cooled off as quickly as it heated up. The group badly needs a boost and that starts with Strong fulfilling the first part of Adam Cole's promise for the group's dominance of AEW.

Which AEW star would you like to see included and win a title shot?

Ricky Starks. The most undervalued young star in the company continues to see his stock rise, then fall, then rise again as his story is handled seemingly on a whim. Entering a match like this, outlasting the likes of Chris Jericho, Hook, Powerhouse Hobbs and Brian Cage, and earning a future title shot could help AEW finally realize his fullest potential. Plus, an arrogant Starks vs. a no-nonsense Joe could be boatloads of fun.
