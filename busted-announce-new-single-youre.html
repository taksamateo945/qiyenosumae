Busted Announce New Single 'On What You're On'

Busted have finally announced their first single in 13 years.

The band’s Twitter account dropped the highly anticipated news of their comeback single ‘On What You’re On’ earlier today.

#OnWhatYoureOn will be the first single to be taken from 'Night Driver'
Stay tuned… pic.twitter.com/vK5HyzDz5s

— Busted (@Busted) September 18, 2016

The tweet also teased the music video, which we expect will be out very soon!

This is the first single taken from their third studio album ‘Night Driver’.

Fans have already had a taster of the new Busted sound due to the release of album track ‘Coming Home’ earlier this year.

Night Driver will be available on November 11th.
