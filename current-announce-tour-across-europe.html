Against The Current announce a tour across Europe

On Monday, Against The Current hinted at big news coming on Wednesday but no clues as to what that could be…

Europe. Wednesday.

— AGAINST THE CURRENT (@ATC_BAND) January 11, 2016

The big news was… A TOUR!

We'll be back starting next month, Europe! Tickets go on sale Friday at 10:00AM, who's coming out!? pic.twitter.com/Ehz04dYbz5

— AGAINST THE CURRENT (@ATC_BAND) January 13, 2016

22/03 Toulouse, France – La Saint Des Seins
25/02 Zaragoza, Spain – Sala King Kong
26/02 Valencia, Spain – 16 Toneladas
27/02 Madrid, Spain – Independence- Charada Club
28/02 Barcelona, Spain – Sala Boveda
01/03 Paris, France – La Trabendo
02/03 Hasselt, Belgium – Muziekodroom
04/03 Amsterdam, Netherlands – Melkweg
05/03 Munster, Germany – Gleis 22
06/03 Hamburg, Germany – Uebel & Gefaehrlich
08/03 Stockholm, Sweden – Fryshuset
09/03 Copenhagen, Denmark – Vega Small Hall
11/03 Berlin, Germany – Phfclub
12/03 Prague, Czech Republic – Futurum
13/03 Vienna, Austria – Szene
14/03 Munich, Germany – Technikum
16/03 Segrate Milan, Italy – Circolo Magnolia
17/03 Lucerne, Switzerland – Schuur
18/03 Frankfurt, Germany – Batschkapp
19/03 Cologne, Germany – Buerghaus Stollwerck

For more information visit Against The Current’s website or follow them on twitter.
